About This Project

Welcome to the Weather Comparison App — your tool to compare live and historical weather data for cities across the globe. This platform is built with FastAPI, Jinja2, and integrates with Amazon S3 for persistent data storage.

🔍 What it Does

  • 📊 Compares max/min temperatures, wind speeds, and rainy day frequency
  • 📁 Stores weather reports in your S3 bucket under weather/
  • 📈 Visualizes comparisons using beautiful Chart.js bar graphs

🎯 Our Mission

Our goal is to make weather data analysis fun, fast, and interactive — whether you're a developer, student, or data enthusiast. We believe clean visuals and cloud-first design can make even the simplest tools powerful.
